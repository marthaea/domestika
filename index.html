Meaningless Demeanors

Poem by Martha Praise Katusiime

The clouds are blue,

The sky is azure and peaceful.

All I can think about is the tranquility within.

The reminiscent tranquility of childhood.

All these meaningless demeanors I remember.

Jumping in the dust, eating dirt,

Not thinking about what tomorrow would bring.

But it has all come to pass.

Meaningless demeanors no longer reminiscent.

Tomorrow is all I think about

And nothing more than the grandeur,

The grandeur of reality.
